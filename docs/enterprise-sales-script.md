# Enterprise Sales Call Script — 15 Minutes
## ClosersAssist Enterprise ($5,000/mo)

**Your voice:** Direct. No fluff. You know their floor better than they think you do.
You're not selling software — you're giving them a closer who never calls out sick.

---

## Minute 0-2: The Opener

> "Hey [name], thanks for jumping on. You filled out the form — what made you click submit? What's happening on your floor right now?"

Let them talk. Listen for keywords:
- "turnover" → this is a people problem
- "inconsistent" → training problem
- "CRM" → tech frustration
- "volume" → scaling problem

Don't pitch yet. Just listen.

---

## Minute 2-5: Discovery (Ask, Don't Tell)

Ask 2-3 of these. Pick based on what they already said:

> "How many reps on your floor right now?"

> "When a new rep starts, how long until they're actually closing deals?"

> "What happens when a customer throws an objection your rep hasn't heard before?"

> "How many deals did you lose last month that you know a better closer would've caught?"

> "What's the one thing you wish every rep would do consistently?"

**The goal:** Get them to say, out loud, "We're leaving money on the floor." Once they say it, you've got them.

---

## Minute 5-10: The Pitch

> "Here's what we do. We build you an AI closer — a digital employee who knows your scripts, your objections, your pay plan, and your inventory. It sits on your floor, available to every rep, 24/7. It gets better every single week."

> "Here's what you get — $5,000 a month, flat. Unlimited reps. We handle everything: setup, hosting, updates, CRM integration. In two days, your first agent is live. By week two, your reps are closing deals they would've fumbled before."

> "This isn't another tool. Your reps already ignore half the tools you give them. This is a closer in their pocket — on Telegram, Slack, or right on the floor. They type in the objection, they get the handle. They log a deal, it tracks their pay plan. They're stuck, the agent unstucks them."

**Don't oversell.** Let the concept land. Then pause.

---

## Minute 10-13: Proof + Fit

If they're leaning in:

> "We're already doing this for dealerships. One GSM told me his greenest rep closed 3 units in his first week because the agent fed him the right script on every up."

If they're skeptical:

> "Look, I'll be straight with you. We don't take every dealership. Your floor has to actually want to close more deals. If your reps don't use it, it won't work. If they do, it pays for itself in one deal. Probably one mini."

(That last line — "probably one mini" — they'll laugh. It's car speak.)

Check fit:

> "Give me a sense — do your reps actually use the tools you give them, or do they ignore everything that's not in front of their face?"

---

## Minute 13-15: The Close

> "Here's how we start. Month one is 30 days, 30 reps, $5K. If at the end of 30 days your team hasn't closed enough extra deals to cover that, you don't pay month two. Simple."

> "I'll send you the agreement today. We go live in 48 hours. Your rep with the lowest closing ratio? That's who we start with. By Friday they'll have a closer in their ear."

**End with:**
> "Let's do this. You in?"

---

## Objection Handlers

**"$5K is steep"**
> "One deal. One additional deal per month covers this. Your worst rep probably leaves one on the table every Saturday. This covers that gap."

**"We already have a CRM"**
> "So do 100% of our clients. The CRM stores the deal. Our agent closes it. Different job."

**"My reps won't use it"**
> "The ones who want to make more money will. The ones who don't — you already know who they are. This exposes them."

**"Can we try before we commit?"**
> "30 days. If the results aren't there, you walk. I take that risk because I know what this does."

**"Who builds it? Who manages it?"**
> "Me. I built the platform. I train the agent. I push weekly updates. You don't touch a single setting. You just watch your numbers move."

---

## Post-Call

- Send agreement within 1 hour (Stripe invoice or Docusign)
- Ask for: CRM access, pay plan PDF, top 3 scripts, top 5 objections
- Set Trello board link for requests
- Day 1: Agent live. Day 7: First optimization push. Day 30: Review.
